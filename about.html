Ambika Transport Services

Ambika Transporters was founded by Late Shri K.R. Aggarwal, in 1986 to provide people with a friendly, simple, and streamlined approach to taking care of all your shipping needs. We offer a full suite of transportation and logistics services throughout North India.

The combined experience of the Ambika Transporters management team, along with our open and honest approach to communication and customer relations, has shaped Ambika Transporters into who we are today.
At Ambika Transport we’re driven to deliver your freight safely and on time. We pride ourselves on service first, that means being people-oriented. When you call our office we connect you to our staff—not a phone recording—so you get fast, friendly, and efficient service every time. Ambika Transport is committed to treating all members of our industry – drivers, in particular – with the recognition and respect they deserve.
