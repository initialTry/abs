About Us

Ambika Transporters

Ambika Transport Services

Ambika Transporters was founded by Late Shri K.R. Aggarwal, in 1986 to provide people with a friendly, simple, and streamlined approach to taking care of all your shipping needs. We offer a full suite of transportation and logistics services throughout North India.

The combined experience of the Ambika Transporters management team, along with our open and honest approach to communication and customer relations, has shaped Ambika Transporters into who we are today.
At Ambika Transport we’re driven to deliver your freight safely and on time. We pride ourselves on service first, that means being people-oriented. When you call our office we connect you to our staff—not a phone recording—so you get fast, friendly, and efficient service every time. Ambika Transport is committed to treating all members of our industry – drivers, in particular – with the recognition and respect they deserve.

Ambika Tour & Travel Services

Ambika Tour & Travel , is a company with 27 years of experience in arranging top quality India tours, Wildlife safaris and Beach holidays in the Indian sub-continent. Our Company presently is headed by R.K Aggarwal who have almost 35+ years of experience of arranging tours in the Indian subcontinent. Each one of our 30 + operations staff is dedicated to providing the highest quality Indian holidays, from designing the itineraries in consultation with you, right up to giving you expert advice on things like health and safety.

Since 1990 we have arranged and handled several thousand India tours for our clients, many of whom have travelled more than once with us. We pride ourselves in saying that "we are small enough to care, and large enough to deliver". Our team of professionals will do everything possible to ensure that your holiday in the Indian sub-continent works like clockwork, despite the challenges of travelling in a continent, not known for its efficient infrastructure.

Get in touch with us, and allow us to create your Indian experience for you.
